Maria House Location

Welcome to Maria House! We are conveniently located in the bustling town of Bunia, Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC). Our store is situated in the heart of the city, making it easily accessible to locals and visitors alike.

Whether you're strolling through the vibrant streets of Bunia or navigating your way from neighboring areas, finding Maria House is simple. Look out for our bright and inviting storefront, adorned with the latest arrivals and stylish displays.

At Maria House, we pride ourselves on providing a welcoming and comfortable shopping experience for our customers. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to assist you in finding the perfect items for your home or wardrobe.

Come visit us today and explore our extensive range of home furnishings, fashion accessories, and more. We look forward to seeing you at Maria House!
